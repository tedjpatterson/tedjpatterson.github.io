Being raised at the feet of the Smokies, eyes constantly focused on peaks in the distance, it didn't take long for me to start wondering what and where else there was to experience in our world.

As for why I'm into interactive design and code, about seven years back (and after taking this progressive oath that I mention up there) I stumbled into photography and graphic design work while living in and traveling through South Korea, India, New Zealand, and China. Three years ago, due to contract and personal design projects, I started to develop a interest in interactive art. This interst was a needed initial push and I started to take on programming classes, assignments, and projects. Fast forward to now and I can say that this skill set, coupled with my ability to envision and craft quality designs and products, has allowed me to tackle and capitalize on a wide collection of opportunities alongside an amazing variety of companies, startups, and teams.

These days I'm currently residing in the Memphis, Tennessee and taking on UX/UI design and web/software development projects at a company called Transnetyx and side projects from time to time.

If you're looking to build something new or refine a design or application that's already in action or just interested in trading stories over a pint, feel free to preview some of work and shoot a message my way.
